Grandma's Secret Apple Pie



This apple pie recepe has been in my favorite for generations and never fails to delight.

Ingridients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples,peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F(190 degree C)
  2. In a bowl,mix the sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Toss the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mixture
